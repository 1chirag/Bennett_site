India's Top University

Bennett University believes that while the career of a student is the prime focus, a university stands for its quality of research. The philosophy of research at BU shall be to contribute in finding sustainable solutions to problems of the modern world. Research at the Bennett University will be nurtured through various academic programs run by the Schools /Departments and through sponsored projects/programs funded by national and international organizations, including the corporate sector/industry.
